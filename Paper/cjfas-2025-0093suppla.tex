The study evaluated the effects of recruitment variability ($\sigma_{Rec}$ = 0.5, 1, 1.5) and $NAA$ variability ($\sigma_{NAA}$ = 0.1, 0.2, ... 0.6) in a factorial design through self-tests and cross-tests.
